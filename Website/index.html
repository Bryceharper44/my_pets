Ive decided to try my hand at building a website, and what better topic than my pets? Together with my girlfriend, we have a full and lively household made up of two cats and two chihuahuas. Its a conjoined family where every pet brings their own unique personality and charm to the mix.

Our oldest is Mordi, a long-haired chihuahua who, at 9 years old, feels like hes from another era compared to the younger ones. Then theres Soul, our relaxed brown tabby who came into our lives during the pandemic and is now 5. Ike Jr., a spirited chihuahua-papillon mix, shares a birthday week with me, turning 3 this past June. And finally, theres Maka, the youngest of the bunch, a 2-year-old with an unknown exact birthdate but a big personality all the same.
